OUR MISSION

To empower the Handloom weavers / handicraft artisans with awareness, required hard and soft skills, connecting them with the buyers digitally as well as provide them required support for making quality, defect free fabrics with new design and color products and sale it online “D” to “C” (Direct to Customer ) which will increase their earnings substantially and attract the younger generation to continue in the traditional occupation.
